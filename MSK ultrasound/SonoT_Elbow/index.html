    SonoT 超音波報告範本 — Elbow

    範本 — Normal Elbow

    >MSUS of the right elbow was performed. >No synovial proliferation, joint effusion was noted. >No tenosynovitis of common extensor tendon or common flexor tendon. >Please correlate with clinical condition.

    範本 — RA Elbow（含 Bone Erosion）

    >MSUS of the left elbow was performed. >Hyperechoic area over olecranon fossa, radiohumeral joint, ulnohumeral joint, with no power doppler signals(-), compatible with synovitis. More prominent synovial hypertrophy than previous study. >Bone erosion (+) over anterior aspect of the ulnohumeral joint. >Common extensor tendon: hyperechoic area around the tendon, no entheseal bone erosion(-), PD signal(-). >Unremarkable findings over Triceps brachii tendon, Common flexor tendon. >Please correlate with clinical condition.

    範本 — Partial Tear of Common Extensor Tendon（Lateral Epicondylitis）

    >MSUS of the right elbow and right wrist were performed. >No synovial proliferation, joint effusion was noted. >Common extensor tendon: hyperechoic area between common extensor tendon and lateral epicondyle, compatible with partial tear of right common extensor tendon. >No compression of right median nerve at carpal tunnel level. >Please correlate with clinical condition.

    範本 — Olecranon Bursitis（含抽吸）

    >MSUS of the left elbow was performed. >Heterogeneous fluid with trabeculae posterior to the left olecranon was noted, with Doppler signal(+) at adjacent soft tissue, compatible with olecranon bursitis. >Under sono-guidance, a 22G needle was inserted into left olecranon bursa, and 5 ml yellowish fluid was aspirated. The patient tolerated this procedure well. >No synovial proliferation, joint effusion was noted. >No tenosynovitis of common extensor tendon or common flexor tendon. >Please correlate with clinical condition.

    範本 — Joint Space Narrowing / Bone Erosion（建議 MRI）

    IMPRESSION:

    Joint space narrowing, especially humeral-ulnar joint. With suspicious bone erosion in Trochlear notch. Suggest MRI for further survey.

    OTHER DESCRIPTION:

    Anterior view: > Intact bone cortex of radius head but irregular cortex of Trochlear notch, suspected bone erosion changes

    Lateral view: > Relative round structure in Capitulum-radius joint.

    Medial view: > Joint space narrowing of humeral-ulnar joint.

    Posterior view: > Normal findings of olecranon fossa.
