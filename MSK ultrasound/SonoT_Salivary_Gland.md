# SonoT 超音波報告範本 — Salivary Gland

## 病例範本 — SjS（OMERACT Gr. 2–3）

**IMPRESSION:**

Heterogenous glands w/ anechoic/hypoechoic areas at bil. parotid & submandibular glands, suspect SjS.
Active inflammation at bil. submandibular gland.

**OTHER DESCRIPTION:**

MSUS of the bilateral parotid glands and submandibular glands was performed.

Rt parotid gland
A heterogenous hyperechoic gland with focal hypoechoic areas was found at the preauricular region along the posterior surface of the mandible. (Omeract Gr. 2)
The length of the gland was about 3.21 cm.
A lymph node was found at the preauricular region with length about 0.53 cm.

Rt submandibular gland
A heterogenous hyperechoic gland with anechoic/hypoechoic areas occupying the entire gland surface and Doppler signal was found at the submandibular triangle. (Omeract Gr. 3)

Lt parotid gland
A heterogenous hyperechoic gland with focal hypoechoic areas was found at the preauricular region along the posterior surface of the mandible. (Omeract Gr. 2)
The length of the gland was about 2.91 cm.
A lymph node was found at the preauricular region with length about 0.83 cm.

Lt submandibular gland
A heterogenous hyperechoic gland with anechoic/hypoechoic areas occupying the entire gland surface and Doppler signal was found at the submandibular triangle. (Omeract Gr. 3)

## 範本 — Normal（Gr. 0）

MSUS of the bilateral parotid glands and submandibular glands was performed.

Normal appearance of Rt parotid gland (OMERACT Gr. 0) and Lt parotid gland (OMERACT Gr. 0).
Normal appearance of bilateral submandibular glands.
Lymph nodes with normal appearance were found at the left preauricular regions.
Please correlate with clinical condition.

---

## 範本 — Mild Inhomogeneity（Gr. 1）

MSUS of the bilateral parotid glands and submandibular glands was performed.

Mild inhomogeneity without anechoic or hypoechoic areas and hyperechogenic bands at Rt parotid gland (OMERACT Gr. 1) and Lt parotid gland (OMERACT Gr. 1).
Normal appearance of bilateral submandibular glands.
Lymph nodes with normal configuration were found at the right and left preauricular regions.
Please correlate with clinical condition.
