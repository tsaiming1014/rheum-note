\>MSUS of the bilateral shoulders and right-hand CMC was performed.
\>Hypoechoic, compressible area above right biceps brachii-long head tendon, with compression to the tendon, compatible with tenosynovitis.
\>Hyperechoic lesions in bilateral subscapularis tendon, compatible with calcifications.
\>Synovial hypertrophy without increased Doppler signals in bilateral acromioclavicular joints, compatible with arthritis.
\>Hypoechoic and heterogenous area within the bilateral supraspinatus tendon, compatible with partial tear of supraspinatus tendon.
\>Unremarkable infraspinatus tendon, teres minor tendon.
\>Right CMC joint osteophytes, suspect degenerative change.
