### 範本 5 — 複雜多發性關節炎 + CTS

\>MSUS of both wrists and hands were done.
\>Synovitis was noted over bil radiocarpal joint (GS2) and bil ulnocarpal joint (GS2)
\>Thickened and increased hypoechoic area around the tendons in left 1st compartment (EPB&APL), bil extensor common digitorum tendon, bil extensor carpi ulnaris tendon, compatible with tenosynovitis
\>Hypoechoic area was found at right MCP 2-3 (GS1), right MCP 2-3 (GS2), right MCP 4 (GS1), compatible with arthritis.
\>Thickened and increased hypoechoic area around the flexor tendon of right digit 2-3, and left digit 2,4,5, suspect tenosynovitis of flexor digitorum tendon.
\>Tendon swelling, sheath distension, heterogenous synovium over extensor tendon of right digit 3-5, left digit 1-4, suspect tendinitis of extensor digitorum tendon.
\>Thickened and increased hypoechoic area around the extensor tendon of right digit 2-3, suspect tenosynovitis of extensor digitorum tendon.
\>Hypoechoic and thickened enthesis insertion of central slip over right PIP 1-4, and left PIP 2-4 with increased doppler signal, suspect central slip enthesitis.
\>Hypoechoic enlargement of right median nerve with thickening of the overlying flexor retinaculum with increased doppler signal, suspect right carpal tunnel syndrome.
\>Please correlate to clinical condition.
