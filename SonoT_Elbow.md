## 範本 — RA Elbow（含 Bone Erosion）

\>MSUS of the left elbow was performed.
\>Hyperechoic area over olecranon fossa, radiohumeral joint, ulnohumeral joint, with no power doppler signals(-), compatible with synovitis. More prominent synovial hypertrophy than previous study.
\>Bone erosion (+) over anterior aspect of the ulnohumeral joint.
\>Common extensor tendon: hyperechoic area around the tendon, no entheseal bone erosion(-), PD signal(-).
\>Unremarkable findings over Triceps brachii tendon, Common flexor tendon.
\>Please correlate with clinical condition.

---

## 範本 — Partial Tear of Common Extensor Tendon（Lateral Epicondylitis）

\>MSUS of the right elbow and right wrist were performed.
\>No synovial proliferation, joint effusion was noted.
\>Common extensor tendon: hyperechoic area between common extensor tendon and lateral epicondyle, compatible with partial tear of right common extensor tendon.
\>No compression of right median nerve at carpal tunnel level.
\>Please correlate with clinical condition.

---

## 範本 — Olecranon Bursitis（含抽吸）

\>MSUS of the left elbow was performed.
\>Heterogeneous fluid with trabeculae posterior to the left olecranon was noted, with Doppler signal(+) at adjacent soft tissue, compatible with olecranon bursitis.
\>Under sono-guidance, a 22G needle was inserted into left olecranon bursa, and 5 ml yellowish fluid was aspirated. The patient tolerated this procedure well.
\>No synovial proliferation, joint effusion was noted.
\>No tenosynovitis of common extensor tendon or common flexor tendon.
\>Please correlate with clinical condition.

---
