#### 零散考點

1. **JIA uveitis risk factor**：younger age、female gender、oligoarticular disease、presence of ANA → chronic anterior uveitis；male with HLA-B27 and ERA → acute anterior uveitis
2. **APS treatment in pregnancy**：aspirin、heparin、plaquenil
3. **Drug of choice in GIOP**：Ca+D；oral bisphosphonates 為 first-line；高風險患者可更早用 teriparatide
4. **Mortality comparison between iPAH and CTD-PAH**
5. **RTX depletes which part of B cell lineage**：Pre-B
6. **PML and JC virus**：JCV reactivation in immunocompromised；JCV 70–80% 人類潛伏感染；目前無有效治療；natalizumab 在 MS 治療中增加 PML 風險
7. **Gout criteria, scoring system**（2015 ACR/EULAR）
   - Entry criteria：至少一次關節/黏液囊腫脹、疼痛或觸痛
   - Sufficient criteria：有症狀關節見 MSU 結晶或痛風石
   - Criteria：≥ 8 分診斷
   - 急性發作：< 24 小時達最嚴重、14 天內緩解、間歇期恢復原始狀態
   - **驗尿酸時機：急性發作四周後**，發作時可能假性偏低！
8. **Neonatal lupus**：incidence ~2%（母親有 Ro/SSA 或 La/SSB）；80–95% 嚴重 AV block；第三度心臟傳導阻滯通常不可逆；IVIG + corticosteroids 可能預防 cardiomyopathy
9. **CYC cumulative dose and teratogenicity**：< 20 歲 → < 15–20 g；20–30 歲 → < 15 g；> 30 歲 → < 10 g；男性 → < 10 g
10. **Acute rheumatic fever**
    - Major criteria：多關節炎、心臟炎、皮下結節、erythema marginatum、Sydenham's chorea
    - Minor criteria：arthralgia、fever ≥ 38.5°C（低風險）/ ≥ 38°C（高風險）、elevated ESR/CRP、ECG prolonged PR interval
